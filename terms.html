Carelift is a technology platform that connects organizers — the people who create campaigns — with donors — the people who choose to support them. Our role is to host campaigns, process donations through trusted payment partners, and give both sides the tools to tell and discover real stories.

It's important to understand what Carelift is not. We are not a charity, a broker, a financial institution, or a creditor. We don't endorse any campaign, and we don't guarantee that a campaign will reach its goal, that funds will be used as described, or that any particular outcome will follow from your donation. Decisions about whether to give, and how much, are entirely yours.

Donations are made to the organizer, not to Carelift. When you give, you are choosing to support a specific person or cause based on the information they've shared. Carelift simply makes that connection possible.

We work hard to keep Carelift safe — reviewing campaigns, responding to reports, and partnering with payment processors that screen for fraud — but no platform can verify every detail of every story. Please use your own judgment, and tell us if something doesn't seem right.

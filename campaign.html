Maria is 38, a mother of two, and a kindergarten teacher in a small town near Lisbon. Last September she was diagnosed with stage III breast cancer.

Maria's treatment plan covers six rounds of chemotherapy followed by surgery and radiation. Her medical insurance covers most of the procedure — but the supplemental therapies, transportation to the cancer center 90km away, and time off work add up to over $25,000 her family simply doesn't have.

Maria's sister Marta started this campaign because watching someone you love fight for their life shouldn't also mean watching them drown in bills. Every dollar raised goes directly to medical costs and to keeping the family afloat while Maria recovers.

"I want my kids to remember this year as the year mom got better," Maria wrote. "Not the year we lost everything trying."
